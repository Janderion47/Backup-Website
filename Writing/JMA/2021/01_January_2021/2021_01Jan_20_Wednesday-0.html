Thought 1

I am now realizing that one of my previous posts here, I think it was one of the texts from over the break, that my thoughts on ethics and how we create our ethical thinking, can be better understood by using the new vocabulary of ethics that we have learned in philosophy yesterday.

One of my previous ideas on ethics is that our ethics would be built on one foundational idea or set of ideas, which I will now refer to as the primary principle or principles. They are the ideas of which we would build up our other ideas, which it would seem appropriate to refer to as secondary principles. Now it would be good to follow the principles, it is good to remain in accordance with all of the principles, and it is bad if your actions are in conflict with the principles. All of our ethics would be based on these primary principles.
